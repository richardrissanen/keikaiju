Mission

Keikaiju is a new car club that brings kei enthusiasts together. We socialize, swap stories, and help each other keep these little strange beasts on the road.

We organize regular meetups and drive-ins to our local car shows, show off our builds, trade tips, and enjoy our kei cars together.
