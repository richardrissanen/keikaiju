Our Mission

Kei.Social exists to bring kei car owners and fans together — to socialize, swap stories and parts, and help each other keep these little legends on the road. Whether you've got a Cappuccino, an AZ-1, a Hijet, or you just love everything about the 660cc world, there's a seat for you here.

Drive-Ins & Car Shows

We organize regular drive-ins, casual meetups, and trips to local car shows so members can show off their builds, trade tips, and just enjoy the cars together. No car too small, no mod too weird — if it's kei, it belongs in the lineup.
