Mission

Keikaiju is a new car club that brings kei enthusiasts together. We socialize, swap stories, and help each other keep these little strange beasts on the road.

Invasions

We organize regular meetups and drive-ins to our local car shows so members can show off their builds, trade tips, and enjoy their kei cars together.
