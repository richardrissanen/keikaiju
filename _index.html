Our Mission

Keikaiju is a car club that brings kei car enthusiasts together. We socialize, swap stories, and help each other keep these little strange beasts on the road.

Drive-Ins & Car Shows

We organize regular drive-ins, casual meetups, and trips to local car shows so members can show off their builds, trade tips, and just enjoy the cars together. No car is too small, and no mod is too weird. If it's kei, it belongs in the lineup.
